About Alice

Alice, or 'Alis, short for Glacialis, is the encompassing name for a cat with three Facets. Aurora, Athame and Aeon. Existing on a scale of presentation, these facets primarily embody light, mist and shadow along with a strong affinity for ice and snow. Several other traits fluctuate along this scale - as a whole, Alice is genderfluid demigirl/agender, and uses e/eir pronouns (pronounced e as in bee, and eir like ear.) Each facet also uses a different secondary set of pronouns too. E is asexual as a contrast to the majority of Monochrome.

Eir favorite place on earth is Iceland, especially Reynisfjara, the black sand beach.

About Aurora

I am the lightcat. You may call me Aurora, Day or Nadia, and use she/her as an alternative to e/eir. I am the most forward and politely social of the cats, and do try to be helpful when I can. I have my soft spots and can at times have my catty needs for affection.


About Athame

I am the intermediate state of cat, in many senses. I go by the first names Athame, Dawn or Ashley (Ash). My auxiliary pronouns are they/them. As given by my primary name, I do quite like knives and sharp things. I am the more feisty of the three cats and enjoy sociality but can show more Aeon-like aversive leanings at times, especially when bothered somehow.


About Aeon

I am the shadowy flavor of cat. I go by the first names Aeon, Dusk, and Ace, and use auxiliary it/its pronouns. My primary sense of self revolves around serenity and solitude. I will gladly take time to myself over situations that don't appeal to me, and have a generally quiet nature. I can still succeed in a quiet comfort around people.
